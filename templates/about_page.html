About the Integrated Career Toolkit

Our mission is to empower job seekers by providing a powerful, all-in-one suite of AI-driven tools to enhance resumes, analyze digital profiles, and prepare for interviews.

Our Vision

To bridge the gap between candidate qualifications and employer expectations using intelligent analysis, making the job search process more transparent and effective.

Key Features

  • **ATS Scorer:** Maximize resume keyword matching.
  • **AI Question Generator:** Prepare for interviews based on your specific skills.
  • **Profile Dashboard:** Synchronize your resume with your LinkedIn presence.
  • **Plagiarism Check:** Ensure your resume is unique and professional.

Developed with Flask, Python, and modern machine learning technologies.
